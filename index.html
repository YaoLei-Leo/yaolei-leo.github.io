About Me:

I am a computation biologist trained in biology and bioinformatics. My research focus on genomics and transcriptomics analysis on sytemic lupus erythematosis (SLE). I am currently interested in integrating multi-omics data to investigate the pathogenic mechanisms underlying genes identified in GWAS and rare-variant association study (RVAS). I am also keen to developing statistical & Machine Learning methods to mitigate batch effects issue in whole-genome sequencing (WGS) data.


Currently, I am in the final year of my Ph.D. and open to job opportunities. Please feel free to contact me if interested.
